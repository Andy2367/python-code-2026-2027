PARENT NOTES
Grades K – 2 are served with a tray which includes a choice of Main Meal, Pasta/Rice or Panini.  Also included are vegetables, potatoes and desert (fruit only).  However, if the Main Meal, Pasta/Rice or Panini is not selected children must ask for an alternative and a Baked Potato With Filling or Soup will be offered.
